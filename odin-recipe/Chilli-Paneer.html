Chilli-Paneer

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 3 tbsp cornflour, 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  • Salt, to taste

For sauce:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chillies, finely chopped (adjust according to your spice level)
  • 1 onion, cut into cubes,1 bell pepper (capsicum), cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp red chilli sauce (adjust according to your spice level)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp spring onion greens, chopped for garnish

Description

Chilli Paneer is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that is made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) that is coated with a spicy batter and then fried until crispy. The crispy paneer cubes are then tossed in a flavorful sauce made with ginger, garlic, onions, bell peppers and a variety of sauces like soy sauce, tomato ketchup, and red chilli sauce. The dish is garnished with spring onion greens and served hot as an appetizer or as a side dish with fried rice or noodles.

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix cornflour, red chilli powder and salt. Add paneer cubes to this mixture and coat them well.
  2. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Once the oil is hot, add the coated paneer cubes and fry until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove the fried paneer cubes onto a paper towel to absorb any excess oil.
  3. In another pan, heat 2 tbsp of oil over medium-high heat. Add finely chopped garlic, ginger and green chillies and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add cubed onions and bell peppers and sauté for 3-4 minutes until they are slightly soft
  5. Add tomato ketchup, soy sauce, vinegar, red chilli sauce, salt and black pepper to the pan and stir well.Add the fried paneer cubes to the sauce and toss well to coat them evenly.Cook for 1-2 minutes until the paneer cubes are well coated with the sauce.Garnish with chopped spring onion greens and serve hot with fried rice or noodles.
  6. Enjoy your delicious Chilli Paneer!
